Firstly here is some information about us.

It all started with the first Lemon Tree in our Grandma's Yard. This was the source of the lemons used in the Pie.

  • First we started as a family enjoying the pies together.
  • Then someone in the family had an idea to start selling pies for the town.
  • The town loved the Pies and we continued to supply them with it.
  • For many years we continued to make these pies and built a strong following in town for them.
  • Eventually we decided to put the recipe online for EVERYONE to enjoy!

  • For the Future we hope to branch out to other pies.

THANKS for visiting our website

I hope you ENJOY the pie just as many others have!
